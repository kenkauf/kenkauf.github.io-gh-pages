Who is Ken?

Everyone has a story... here's mine.

I grew up in a tiny town tucked away on Lake Michigan. From an early age, I played guitar and developed a passion for music. To satisfy my creativity, I wrote music and DJ'd parties. Mentored by my father, I observed as he built his business from the ground up. Watching this process taught me strategic business practices and the power of empathetic leadership.

When college arrived, I imagined my future embodying passion, creation, and empathy. As a senior at the University of Michigan's School of Information, I'm finishing my studies on User Experience Design and Research in April. Technology evolves radically and as a designer, I realize the importance of empowering the end user. Let me help make your users' lives easier. Hope you enjoy my site, please reach out if want to learn more. Cheers!
